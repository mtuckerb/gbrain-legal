Γbrain — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Γbrain ("the app") is a native shell that loads a privately hosted web application ("the service") operated by an individual, Tucker Bradford. The app itself does not independently collect, transmit, or sell any data — it opens the service inside a native window/WebView and provides optional native conveniences (persistent sign-in, drag-and-drop file import/export). This policy covers both the app and the service it connects to.

Who this is for

The service is a personal AI assistant operated for its owner and a small number of invited testers as part of Apple TestFlight external beta testing. It is not a general-purpose public product at this time.

What data is collected

  • Account/session data: a signed session identifier used to keep you signed in.
  • Content you provide: messages, files, and notes you send to or store in the service while using it.
  • Basic technical data: standard web request logs (e.g. timestamps, error logs) used for operating and debugging the service.

The native app additionally stores, only on your device: your selected server address and native WebView session/cache data, so you stay signed in between launches. This data never leaves your device except as part of normal requests to the service you configured.

How data is used

Data you provide is used solely to operate the service for you — for example, to generate AI responses to your messages, or to store and retrieve files you upload. Some requests are relayed to third-party AI providers (such as Anthropic) strictly to generate a response to your request; those providers process the request under their own terms and are not permitted to use it to advertise to you.

What is not done with your data

Data is not sold, rented, or used for advertising. There is no ad tracking or third-party analytics SDK in the app.

Data retention and control

Your content is retained for as long as your account is active so the service can function (e.g. so you can retrieve past notes or conversations). You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting the address below.

Children's privacy

This service is not directed to children and is not knowingly used by children.

Contact

Questions about this policy or requests regarding your data: privacy@tuckerbradford.com

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the "Last updated" date above will change.
